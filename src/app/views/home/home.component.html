NFT SOLUTION

Zyfty creates private, transferrable NFTs representing ownership of long-term lease contracts of up to 99 years.

This simple solution eliminates any difficulty in transferring ownership of the NFT because title never transfers, but the NFT still offers the benefits of ownership.

Zyfty NFTs may be sold and resold on the Zyfty Marketplace at a fraction of traditional real estate market time and cost.

NFT BENEFITS

Privacy with Ownership

Zyfty is the title holder of record, thus securing the NFT owner’s privacy while providing the benefits of home ownership.

Fast Transactions

Zyfty NFTs can transfer instantaneously with a transaction cost as little as 0.5%.

Liquidity

Zyfty NFTs allow fast, inexpensive transactions and have exposure the the DeFi ecosystem.
